About

Twenty-five years behind the camera, now directing generative AI for brands.

I’m a photographer by trade and built most of my career in London, UK, in the editorial and advertising industry, before relocating to the French West Indies.

I’ve worked with a diverse range of celebrities, from film stars like Liv Tyler and Sean Bean to musicians like Robbie Williams, as well as comedians, TV presenters and sports stars. On the advertising side, I’ve had the opportunity to work for brands such as H&M, Rimmel London or L’Oréal, to name a few.

I started experimenting with AI, pushing my craft in new directions. I got hooked straight away. Now it’s another tool in my creative kit, one more way to create high-end visuals for my clients.
